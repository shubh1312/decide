How to choose

Fast call

Pick Tropika when

The birthday plan is more group outing than quiet dinner. It wins on craft beer, scale, variety, and memorable atmosphere.

Pick Beige when

The team wants a calmer rooftop evening with live music, attentive service, and a tighter food experience.

Most likely answer

For a team lead birthday with a mixed team, Tropika is the safer default. Book Beige if the guest of honor prefers quieter places.
